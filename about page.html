About Campus Helper

Campus Helper is a student-focused digital assistant created to simplify campus life. It was developed as a mini-project by Computer Science students who wanted to bring all essential services—like notices, reminders, and lost & found—into one easy-to-use platform.

🌟 Why Campus Helper?

Every student faces difficulties in keeping track of important updates, remembering deadlines, and finding lost belongings. Campus Helper was created to solve these real problems and ensure smoother communication within the college community.

🚀 Key Features

  • 📢 Digital Notice Board for announcements
  • ⏰ Reminders for exams, assignments, and events
  • 🔎 Lost & Found section to report and find belongings
  • 📍 Easy navigation to essential campus resources

👨‍💻 Development Team

This project was designed and developed by enthusiastic 3rd Year CSE students. We collaborated to brainstorm, design, and code this project with the goal of solving everyday student problems in a practical way.
